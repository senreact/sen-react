## Question 3 — The long-term vision

We are designing Sen React so that **after hand-off, your team can maintain and evolve the platform without needing a developer for routine work**.

In the medium term, as REACT grows in AI and digital literacy (aligned with your AI FOR CHANGE ACTORS programme), your team can subscribe to AI coding tools and evolve the platform through natural-language conversation — adding features, fixing issues, designing new pages.

This is the opposite of how most web projects work (where the developer stays permanently needed). We believe it aligns with REACT's mission of building autonomy and literacy in Senegal.

**We need to know**:

- Does this vision align with how you see REACT's future?
- Is there anything you would **add** or **change** about this approach?
- Do you see any part of REACT that would need permanent developer support (not self-maintainable), and if so, what?
