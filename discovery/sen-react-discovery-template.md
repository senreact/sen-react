---

## How to answer

- Write freely. Bullet points, short paragraphs, one-word answers — all fine.
- You can answer in **French or English** — whichever is more natural for a given question.
- Examples are provided in *italics* only to help you — ignore them if your reality is different.
- If a question reveals a feature we haven't discussed, **add it to the relevant section**. This is your platform.

## Section 4 — Services (what the platform does for users)

Each service deserves a dedicated deep-dive. Please answer each block.

### 4.1 Information Dissemination

1. What *kind* of information gets disseminated? (news, analyses, reports, alerts, market prices?)
2. Where does this information come from? (REACT team writes it? Partners contribute? Government feeds? Aggregated from other sites?)
3. How often does it get updated? (Daily? Weekly? When something happens?)
4. Should users be able to subscribe (email newsletter? WhatsApp? RSS?) to get information pushed to them?

### 4.2 Business Formalisation Support

This is a major feature — Senegal has specific agencies (ADEPME, APIX, NINEA, RCCM, CNSS, IPRES). Please help us understand the real flow.

1. **Step by step** — what are the actual steps to formalise a business in Senegal today? (Walk us through as if we were a young entrepreneur starting from zero.)
2. Which of those steps can Sen React **help directly** (provide a tutorial, a downloadable form, a checklist)?
3. Which steps need **external redirection** (link to government site or physical agency)?
4. Should we offer **personalised guidance** (a wizard that asks questions about the entrepreneur and recommends the right formalisation path)?
5. Should we offer **1-on-1 support** (user requests help, REACT team follows up by email/WhatsApp/call)?
6. Are there existing guides, PDFs, or videos that we can reuse, or do we need to create everything from scratch?
7. Is there a status / progress tracker users would find useful ("I've done steps 1-3, next is step 4")?

### 4.3 B2B Networking — deep dive

This is one of the two biggest open questions. Please answer with as much detail as you can.

1. **Who connects to whom?** Entrepreneurs to entrepreneurs? Entrepreneurs to suppliers? Entrepreneurs to buyers? Entrepreneurs to service providers (lawyers, accountants)? All of the above?
2. **Profile depth** — when someone creates a B2B profile, what information should it contain? (Company name, sector, description, products/services, photos, contact details, certifications, testimonials, years in operation?)
3. **Discovery** — how do users find each other? Browse a directory? Search by sector / region? Get recommended matches? Attend virtual events?
4. **Contact / communication** — when A wants to reach B, what happens?
    - See phone / email on profile and contact externally?
    - Send a message inside Sen React (in-platform inbox)?
    - Request an introduction that REACT facilitates?
5. **Trust & verification** — should profiles be verified (by REACT, by document upload, by partner endorsement)? Reviews? Ratings?
6. **Differentiation** — what separates a "listed business" from a "verified business" or "premium business"?
7. **Post of needs** — should users be able to post "I am looking for X" (a tender, a request for quote, a hiring call) and receive responses?
8. **Monetisation later** — are you open to paid premium listings / boosted visibility down the line to fund the platform? (Answer won't change what we build now — only informs us.)

### 4.4 Community Networking

1. How is this **different** from B2B networking? (Our hypothesis: Community = informal peer support, discussion, events. B2B = transactional business connections. Please confirm or correct.)
2. What forms of community interaction do you want?
    - Discussion forums (Q&A by sector)
    - Groups (by region, sector, interest)
    - Events calendar (in-person + online)
    - Live webinars / office hours
    - Peer-to-peer mentorship matching
    - Announcements from REACT
3. Who moderates community content, and how?
4. Should community be **open** (anyone can read), **registered** (must sign up to read / post), or **curated** (by application only)?

### 4.5 Capacity Building

1. What does capacity building mean in practice on Sen React?
    - Self-paced online courses?
    - Downloadable e-books / guides?
    - Live workshops (in-person or online)?
    - Certifications or diplomas?
    - Mentorship matching?
2. Who produces the training content? (REACT team? Partners? External educators?)
3. Is training **free forever**, or could some be paid?
4. Should we track user progress? (Enrolled, completed, certificate issued?)
5. Should we integrate with existing platforms (YouTube, Coursera, Moodle) or host natively?
